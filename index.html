01 — About

A passionate AI student with solid foundations in machine learning, deep learning and software engineering, paired with strong leadership from heading PR & Fundraising at IEEE and INGENIUM E-JUST.

I'm a third-year Computer Science & AI student at the Egypt-Japan University for Science and Technology (E-JUST), on track for First Class Honours. My work spans the full AI stack — building end-to-end Arabic speech recognition, handwritten OCR, medical image segmentation, and generative models.

I enjoy translating messy real-world problems into clean, reliable intelligent systems — and I bring strong communication, teamwork and a relentless drive to keep learning.
